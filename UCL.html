UEFA Champions League

What is UEFA Champions League ?

		
The UEFA Champions League (abbreviated as UCL) is an 
annual club football competition organised
by the Union of European Football Associations 
(UEFA) and contested by top-division European clubs,
deciding the best team in Europe. It is one of
the most prestigious tournaments in the world and
the most prestigious club competition in European football,
played by the national league champions
(and, for some nations, one or more runners-up)
of the strongest UEFA national associations.
